About Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns making spaces in 3x3 grid. The player who success in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row win the game. If all nine squares are full without either player acheving a line of tree, the game ends in a draw.

History

Tic Tac Toe dates back to ancient Egypt, where similar games were played on stone surface. the modern version of the game become popular in the 20th century and is now a staple of childhood games worldwide.

Fun Fact

The first computer program to play Tic Tac Toe was developed in 1952 by A.S. Douglas as part of his PhD thesis on human-computer interaction.
